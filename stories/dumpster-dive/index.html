Dumpster Dive

2024-04-22
#equipment  #trash 

Table of Contents

When I moved long time ago, most of my stuff where kind of put in boxes without labels.. as a result, especially those not really critical (never keep that stuff, they become expensive in various terms - time, space, sanity) they become forgotten and clutter.

So far I’ve found

  • old mobile phones
  • network components (switches, ISDN routers, access points)
  • handheld devices
  • hard disks
  • old desktop computers
  • motherboards
  • cables (serial, parallel, scsi, tons of usb-A)
  • PSUs
  • voip phones, ATAs
  • magazines, books, manuals
  • cdroms, floppy disks, even 8mm tapes (films)

pictures, photos, manuals, skus and possibly links to get these will be added later.
